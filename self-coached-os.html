Add to Home Screen

iOS doesn't have a one-tap install button, but it works the same way once you've added it.

  1. Tap the Share icon at the bottom of Safari (square with an up-arrow).
  2. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  3. Tap Add. The app icon will appear on your home screen — open it from there and it runs fullscreen, offline.

Works in Safari. Chrome on iOS uses Apple's WebKit too — same steps via its Share menu.
